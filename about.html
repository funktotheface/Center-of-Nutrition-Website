Helping You Build a Healthier, More Sustainable Lifestyle

Welcome! I’m​ Jake McCullough, a health coach specializing in nutrition, fitness, and mindful living. My mission is to help you understand your body, optimi​se your nutrition, and build sustainable habits that align with your long-term well-being.
I believe that health isn’t just about restrictive diets or punishing workout routines​—it’s about creating a lifestyle that works for you. Through a combination of science-backed nutrition, practical coaching, and real-life strategies, I guide individuals towards a balanced and fulfilling approach to ​optimal health​ and longevity.

My Approach

Nutrition is a science, not an opinion. I take a holistic, individuali​sed approach to wellness, ensuring that each person I work with gets a tailored plan suited to their unique ​goals, ​standards, needs and lifestyle. Whether you're looking to improve energy levels, enhance athletic performance, transition to ​a particular diet, or simply feel better in your own skin , I provide actionable guidance that fits seamlessly into your daily routine.​ Over a decade of research and hands-on experience has made me a strong advocate for the Whole Food, Plant-Based (WFPB) diet—a lifestyle renowned for its longevity-boosting benefits and its potential to reverse many of the world’s most pressing health concerns .​ Not to mention, it's a diet which can be maintained for life. That said, this isn’t about radical changes or overnight conversions. The WFPB diet is often considered the ​diamond standard of nutrition, but at Centre of Nutrition, we take a science-driven, personali​zed approach to dietary shifts. Expect gradual, evidence-based adjustments that support your body’s natural balance—without shock, pressure, or ideology. You also have free access to many of my WFPB resources right here on my website. Whether you're curious or committed, I’m here to guide you through an approach that puts your health first, always.
